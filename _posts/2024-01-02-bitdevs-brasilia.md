**Bitdevs Brasília** is the **local BitDevs chapter I host in Brasília**.

The public site at **`bitdevs.bsb.br`** works as the main coordination surface for the group: it publishes meetup pages, keeps an archive of past and future sessions, and gives each seminar a simple permanent home on the web.

What makes the project worth listing here is that it is not just an event landing page. It is part of the ongoing work of building a **technical Bitcoin community in Brasília**, with recurring socratic seminars, reading material, and a public record that lowers the cost of showing up and staying involved.

This project entry uses **2024-01-02**, the public creation date of the `ClubeBitcoinUnB/bitdevs.bsb.br` repository.
